How to change a lightbulb

Audio background music for motivation

Materials

  • 1 Lamp
  • 1 Replacement lightbulb
  • 1 Pair of elastic gloves
  • 4 Papertowels
  • 1 Rubber hammer

Directions

  1. Locate lamp that needs lightbulb replacement
  2. Make sure the lamp is unplugged from an outlet
  3. Ensure the lightbulb is cool by quickly touching it
  4. Slowly begin to unscrew the lightbulb from the Lamp
  5. Once unscrewed, wrap the four paper towels around the lightbulb
  6. Gently smash the lightbulb, and discard in the trash
  7. Grab the replacement lightbulb
  8. Gently screw in the lightbulb
  9. Make sure the bulb isn't tightened but is fastened into place
  10. Voila!
