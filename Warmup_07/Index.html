The Importance of Family

June 07, 2023

Family is so important. They are the ones who make you the person you become. Sure there are ups and downs but they are the ones who build your character. You are able to confide with them, put your trust in them, and be vulnerable with them. It’s not every day that you find people who will actively and caringly listen to what you have to say. Sometimes there are struggles where you feel as if you don’t understand each other or have a means of communication. But on those days is when you realize that it’s part of having a family. You can’t have everything perfect in life, and struggles like this helps you grow. In my personal experience, I’ve always had a hard time communicating with my family due to a verbal processing disorder and an ongoing TBI that has affected my memory. But over the years, my family has worked extra hard to understand my case. Sometimes through playing music, create art, going on walks, and using our bodies to express how we feel. It helps a lot to have people around you that want to try and help you, understand you, and make you feel important. Especially if they are similar to you, it’s relieving to not have to try so hard or to mask your every move. On special days, for example, a birthday, the family will always try to make you feel important, whether it be an action or putting you on a pedestal all throughout the day. It’s the actions that matter and the true caringness. And you see this caring action to be true because you know them. You know who they are which means you also don’t have to stress. In many cases with friends, relationships can fall apart. But family always prevails, no matter what happens. You stick together because no one will understand you better than your own blood.
