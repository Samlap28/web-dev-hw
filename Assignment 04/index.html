Crepes

Ingredients

  • 1 cup all-purpose flour
  • 2 large eggs
  • ½ cup milk
  • ½ cup water
  • ¼ teaspoon salt
  • 2 tablespoons butter, melted

Directions

  1. Turn on stove to medium hot
  2. Whisk the flour and eggs
  3. Gradually add the milk and water
  4. Scoop the batter onto a hot griddle
  5. Cook until lightly browned on the bottom
  6. Flip and continue cooking until done on both sides
